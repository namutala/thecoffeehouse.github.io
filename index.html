OUR VISION

“Our vision is To Be recognised as THE leading Independent Coffee Operator in Uganda.”

MISSION

We wish to redefine the coffee experience on our forgotten high streets, selecting locations where we feel our presence makes a difference.

OUR VALUES

To take a fair approach in everything that we do, operating with absolute honesty and integrity throughout.

OUR STORY

Originated in the heart of Gayaza Village; mother and daughter
wanted to change the way people Eat, Drink & Relax.


Following its success, we quickly expanded and took
residency on some of the North Wests forgotten High Streets.
All with local regeneration in mind.,
we are preparing for our 22nd store
opening & have many more exciting plans ahead.
